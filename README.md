# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee shop sales performance from **January 2023 to May 2023**. It helps business owners and managers monitor revenue, transactions, product performance, and customer purchasing trends through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![Coffee Shop Dashboard](https://github.com/sudhanshu1903/PowerBI-Project/blob/main/Coffee%20Shop%20Dashboard.png)

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Track overall sales performance.
- Analyze product category revenue contribution.
- Monitor transaction volume trends.
- Identify top-selling product categories.
- Understand monthly sales distribution.
- Compare performance across weekdays.

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Revenue by Product Category
Displays total sales revenue generated by each product category.

**Key Insights:**
- Coffee generated the highest revenue (270K).
- Tea contributed 196K in sales.
- Bakery products generated 82K revenue.

### 2️⃣ Transaction Quantity by Product Category
Shows quantity sold across product categories.

**Top Categories:**
- Coffee – 89K units
- Tea – 70K units
- Bakery – 23K units

### 3️⃣ Transactions by Day Name
Analyzes transaction trends across weekdays.

**Observations:**
- Friday recorded the highest transactions.
- Saturday showed the lowest transaction count.

### 4️⃣ Monthly Sales Distribution
Pie chart showing sales quantity contribution by month.

**Insights:**
- May and June contributed the highest share of sales.
- February recorded the lowest sales volume.
